Sell Your First Prompt Pack

From download to your first sale — no tech skills required

Your licence lets you sell the prompts individually or in bundles at any price — but you may not sell the whole vault as one product, and you may never give the prompts away free. The vault file itself is for your own personal use. This guide shows you how to turn it into income the right way.

1

Open your vault and pick your prompts

Double-click index.html to open your vault. Browse the 100 categories and choose the prompts for your first product — a single high-value prompt, or a themed bundle (e.g. 20 "Instagram Caption" prompts). Tip: pick one clear outcome so the value is obvious.

2

Generate your listing

Open source-files/sell-any-prompt-generator.html in your browser. Paste your prompt, add the outcome, audience and price tier, then click Generate. It builds your title, description, benefits, tags, suggested price, delivery steps and a promo caption — copy each block.

3

Build the deliverable file

Paste the prompt(s) into a clean Google Doc, Google Sheet or Canva PDF. Add your own logo/brand name at the top and one line of instructions: "Paste into ChatGPT, Claude or any AI tool. Replace the [brackets] with your details." Export as a PDF. (Need raw text fast? Use source-files/100k-prompts.csv or the per-category CSVs.)

4

List it on your store

Create a new Digital Download on Etsy, Gumroad, Beacons, Payhip or Stan. Paste in the generated title and description, add the tags, upload your PDF, and set any price you like — just never $0. Add a simple branded cover image (Canva has free templates).

5

Promote it

Post the generated promo caption on Instagram/Threads with a link to your listing. "Comment PROMPT and I'll send the link" works great for single prompts. Drop the prompt's result as proof so people see what it produces.

6

Get paid & scale

Your store delivers the file automatically and you keep 100%. To earn more, bundle related prompts into bigger themed packs and upsell single-prompt buyers to a pack. Repeat for new niches.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Can I sell the whole 100,000-prompt vault?
A: No. Your licence covers selling prompts individually or in bundles only. The complete vault may not be sold or transferred as a single product — it is yours for personal use. See mrr-licence.html.
Q: Can I give prompts away free to grow my list?
A: No. The prompts must never be given away free, including as lead magnets or freebies. They must always be a paid product.
Q: What price should I charge?
A: Anything above $0 — there is no minimum or maximum. Single prompts often sell for $3–$7, small bundles $9–$17, themed packs $19–$39. The generator suggests a range for you.
Q: Do I need any technical skills?
A: No. You only ever copy prompts into a doc and upload a file to your store. No hosting, no code.
Q: Can I rebrand the prompts as my own?
A: Yes. Put your own brand name and logo on the products you create. Just don't claim you wrote the prompts as original work, and don't resell the full vault.
